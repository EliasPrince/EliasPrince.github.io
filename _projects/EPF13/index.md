description:  Structural FEA for U.S. Navy and commercial vessels using Nastran and FEMAP. One of the more complex programs was the EPF-13 Expeditionary Fast Transport, where I developed structural reinforcements to meet manufacturing and code requirements. I also built VBA scripts to automate model setup and post-processing workflows across the team.
